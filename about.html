About Medics on Move

Medics on Move is a professional provider of comprehensive emergency medical and safety services, specializing in pre-hospital emergency care for the film industry and live events of all types and scales. Our highly trained team of medical professionals ensures fast, reliable, and discreet emergency support, tailored to the dynamic and demanding environments of both productions and public gatherings.

In addition to on-site emergency medical coverage, we offer a broad range of complementary services, including:

  • Medical Rescue Services – Equipped to respond swiftly to high-risk or remote incidents, our team is trained in various rescue disciplines to ensure the safety and recovery of individuals in challenging situations.
  • First Aid Training – We provide accredited, hands-on first aid training courses to individuals, groups, and organizations, helping to build life-saving skills and preparedness across communities and workplaces.
  • Fire Safety Services – Our fire safety professionals are equipped to assess, monitor, and manage fire risks, ensuring compliance and readiness for both events and productions.
  • Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) – We deliver expert OHS services, including risk assessments, safety planning, and on-site support, promoting a safe working environment in compliance with regulatory standards.

At Medics on Move, our commitment to clinical excellence, professionalism, and operational flexibility has made us a trusted partner across a wide range of industries. Whether behind the scenes on a film set, at a public event, or within the workplace, we ensure safety, readiness, and peace of mind.
